The Problem

Learning and teaching American Sign Language (ASL) with children is difficult. We recognize if you are either a child who is deaf or hard-of-hearing, but those around you have no prior knowledge of ASL. In addition, you may be a parent of a deaf or hearing-impaired child, but do not know ASL and cannot teach it. We understand commonly available resources are limited in function, variety, and availability.

500,000

ASL is the natural language of 500,000 deaf people in the U.S. and Canada

90%

More than 90% of deaf are born to hearing parents.

Our Solution

A mobile application that will make learning ASL...

Kid-friendly

Simple, colorful, and exciting.

Engaging

Provide a fun and constantly interactive experience.

Accessible

Smartphones/tablets = common resource.

Personalized

Children will be able to readily learn the ASL most relevant to them.

Camera

Snap pictures of surrounding objects to get translations instantly.

ASL Translation

Get back the ASL translation after taking a photo or exploring the dictionary.

Dictionary

Search through the dictionary for translations in categories: ABC's, people, home, food, and animals.

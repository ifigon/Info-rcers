The Problem

Finding resources outside the classroom to teach and learn ASL can be difficult, but especially when…

You are a child who is deaf or hard-of-hearing, but those around you have no prior knowledge of ASL

You are the parent of a deaf or hearing-impaired child, but do not know ASL and cannot teach your child

Commonly available resources are limited due to

  • Limited free content
  • Little interaction with the child’s surroundings
  • No recent updates

Kids are curious, active, and love to learn! So we decided to make an app that caters to these qualities and improves current solutions.

Our Solution

A mobile application that will make learning ASL...

Kid-friendly

Simple, colorful, and exciting.

Engaging

Provide a fun and constantly interactive experience.

Accessible

Smartphones/tablets = common resource.

Personalized

Children will be able to readily learn the ASL most relevant to them.

Camera

Snap pictures of surrounding objects to get translations instantly.

ASL Translation

View the ASL translation after taking a photo or exploring the dictionary.

Dictionary

Search through the dictionary for translations in categories: ABC's, people, home, food, and animals.
